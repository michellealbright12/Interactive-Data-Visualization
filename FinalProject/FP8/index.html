Summary

Our data has 184 attributes (or columns), they are a mix of numeric and categorical values. Most of them are descbribing the land around the lake. We also have a csv file containing the raw data for the chemical readings of each lake, over all the years possible. These data correspond to the lakeID in the original data set, so we can connect the two. We are going to focus on a few things: the geographic location of the lake, the depth and area of the lake, the state that the lake is in, the data on the surrounding area (more on this in a second), and the chemical make-up of the lake.
The data on the surrounding area is given by the column headings begining with nlcd. These data points give the percentage of the land surrounding the lake in question that is categorized in a certain category. The table below describes the columns:
nlcd2011_Pct_11 Percent of land use/cover in the zone from the 2011 NLCD, open water
nlcd2011_Pct_21 Percent of land use/cover in the zone from the 2011 NLCD, Developed, Open Space
nlcd2011_Pct_22 Percent of land use/cover in the zone from the 2011 NLCD, Developed, Low Intensity
nlcd2011_Pct_23 Percent of land use/cover in the zone from the 2011 NLCD, Developed, Medium Intensity
nlcd2011_Pct_24 Percent of land use/cover in the zone from the 2011 NLCD, Developed, High Intensity
nlcd2011_Pct_31 Percent of land use/cover in the zone from the 2011 NLCD, Barren (Rock/Sand/Clay)
nlcd2011_Pct_41 Percent of land use/cover in the zone from the 2011 NLCD, Deciduous Forest
nlcd2011_Pct_42 Percent of land use/cover in the zone from the 2011 NLCD, Evergreen Forest
nlcd2011_Pct_43 Percent of land use/cover in the zone from the 2011 NLCD, Mixed Forest
nlcd2011_Pct_52 Percent of land use/cover in the zone from the 2011 NLCD, Scrub/Shrub
nlcd2011_Pct_71 Percent of land use/cover in the zone from the 2011 NLCD, Grassland/Herbaceous
nlcd2011_Pct_81 Percent of land use/cover in the zone from the 2011 NLCD, Pasture/Hay
nlcd2011_Pct_82 Percent of land use/cover in the zone from the 2011 NLCD, Cultivated Crops
nlcd2011_Pct_90 Percent of land use/cover in the zone from the 2011 NLCD, Woody Wetlands
nlcd2011_Pct_95 Percent of land use/cover in the zone from the 2011 NLCD, Emergent Herbaceous Wetland
As you can see, we are focusing on the 2011 data, but we can also visualize this same data from 2001, or 2006.

Columns we will be using:
  • nhd_lat -> latitude of lake
  • nhd_long -> longitude of lake
  • lake_area_ha -> area of the lake in ha
  • lagosname1 -> name of the lake
  • meandepth -> mean depth of the lake
  • maxdepth -> maximum depth of the lake
  • lagoslakeid -> lakeID connected to the lagos data set
  • summerpud -> photo user days for the summer months. this data is proven to correlate to visitations to the lake. (total from 2001 to 2011)
  • totalpud -> similar to summerpud, but for the whole time period (2001-2011)
  • county_name -> county of the lake
  • state_name -> state that the lake is in
  • nlcd2011_Pct_11 -> see above
  • nlcd2011_Pct_21 -> see above
  • nlcd2011_Pct_22 -> see above
  • nlcd2011_Pct_23 -> see above
  • nlcd2011_Pct_24 -> see above
  • nlcd2011_Pct_31 -> see above
  • nlcd2011_Pct_41 -> see above
  • nlcd2011_Pct_42 -> see above
  • nlcd2011_Pct_43 -> see above
  • nlcd2011_Pct_52 -> see above
  • nlcd2011_Pct_71 -> see above
  • nlcd2011_Pct_81 -> see above
  • nlcd2011_Pct_82 -> see above
  • nlcd2011_Pct_90 -> see above
  • nlcd2011_Pct_95 -> see above
  • avgsecchi -> average secchi depth. this is a measurement of clarity in the water
  • eutrophic -> boolean value: if secchi is below 1.8 the lake is categorized as "eutrophic"
As well as the columns from the raw data that have the information on the chemical data.
